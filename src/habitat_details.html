Building Houses in Evanston Community

Date: February 30th, 2018

Time Committment: 8:00am-3:00pm (7 hours)

Habitat for Humanity offers a wide variety of opportunities to reach homeless individuals in Evanston. Volunteers needed to help move packages and paint homes for 18-24 young adults who were formerly homeless and are currently in need of support while they finish school/job-training.
